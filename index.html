Biography

Kobe Bean Bryant was born on August 23, 1978 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania but raised in Italy in later years. His father's is Joe Bryant former NBA player from Philadelphia 76ers and his mother's name Pam Bryant.

Family

In 2001, Kobe Bryant and Vanessa Laine got married in California and they raised 4 chiildren named, Natalia Diamante born in 2003, Gianna Maria-Onore born in 2006, Bianka born in 2016 and Capri born in 2019.

Career Overview

Kobe Bryant started his high school basketball at Lower Merion High School. He was drafted in 1996 NBA Draft and selected as no.13 overall pick by Charlotte Hornets but traded later that to Los Angeles Lakers he was 18 years old at that time. He started his career with 0 points then ended up his career with 60 points.

1996

Rookie year of Kobe Bryant. He played 71 games and averaged 15.5 mpg, 7.6 pts, 1.9 rebs and 1.3 ast.

1997

Kobe Bryant joined his first slam dunk contest and he became a champhion

2000

Kobe Bryant won his 1st NBA championship after defeating Indiana Pacers with a 4-2. Kobe played 68 games 40.9 mpg, 28.5 pts, 5.9 rebs and 5.0 ast. It is also the start of the dynasty of their dynamic duo with Shaquille O'Neal.

2001

They defeated Philadelphia 76ers with a record of 4-1 to won their 2nd and back-to-back NBA championship trophy. In that season, Kobe Bryant averaged 41.5 mpg, 30.0 pts, 6.9 rebs and 5.9 ast while playing 82 games.

2002

Kobe Bryant won his 3rd NBA championship with Shaq (three-peat) after they defeat New Jersey Nets with a record of 4-0. Kobe averaged 37.6 mpg, 24.0 pts, 5.5 rebs and 5.1 ast in that season. It is their last NBA championship together.

2008

Kobe Bryant won his 1st and only NBA MVP award in this year.

2008

Kobe Bryant won his 1st gold medal in Beijing Summer Olympic Games as a team captain of "Redeem Team". They defeated Spain in the finals, 118-107 in favor of USA.

2009

Kobe Bryant won his 4th NBA championship with his former enemy in Beijing Olympics, Pau Gasol. He was also awarded as NBA Finals MVP after they defeated Orlando Magic with a 4-1 record. He averaged 38.8 mpg, 27.0 pts, 5.4 rebs and 5.0 ast playing 73 games in that season.

2010

Kobe Bryant won his 5th and last and back-to-back NBA championship with Pau Gasol. He was also awarded in his 2nd NBA Finals MVP with a record of 4-3 after getting their revenge with Boston Celtics that defeated them on 2008 NBA Finals. He averaged 33.9 mpg, 25.3 pts, 5.1 rebs and 4.7 ast while playing 82 games.

2012

Kobe Bryant won his 2nd and last gold model in London Olympics after defeating again the Spain in finals, 107-100 in favor of USA.

2014

Kobe Bryant surpassed his idol, mentor and friend Michael Jordan in NBA all-time scoring leaders becoming 3rd at that time.

2016

In this year, Kobe Bryant played his last game against Utah Jazz. Wherein he scored 60 pts, 4 rebs and 4 asts playing 42 mins in his last game as he retire and end his 20 years of career with Los Angeles Lakers.
